Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the AI Lipsync web app?

The AI Lipsync web app is a platform that utilizes advanced artificial intelligence to create realistic lip movements for animated characters based on audio input. It is designed for content creators, developers, and anyone looking to enhance their multimedia projects.

2. How do I get started?

To get started, simply sign up for an account on our website, and follow the user guide available in the documentation section. You can upload audio files and choose animated characters to create your lipsync animations.

3. What formats does the app support?

Our app supports a variety of audio formats, including MP3, WAV, and OGG. Ensure your audio files are of good quality for the best results.

4. Is there a limit to the length of the audio I can upload?

Currently, the maximum audio length you can upload is 5 minutes. For longer recordings, consider splitting them into shorter segments.

5. How can I contact support if I have further questions?

If you have additional questions or need assistance, please visit our Support page for contact options, including our email and contact form.

6. Is the app free to use?

We offer a free tier with basic features, and additional premium features are available through subscription plans. Check our pricing page for more details.

7. Can I use the generated videos commercially?

Yes, you can use the generated videos for commercial purposes. However, please review our terms of service for any specific restrictions.

8. Where can I find the latest updates or announcements?

Stay updated by checking our blog or subscribing to our newsletter for the latest news and feature releases.
